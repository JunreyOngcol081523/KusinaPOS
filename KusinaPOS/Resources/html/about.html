About KusinaPOS

KusinaPOS is a modern, easy-to-use Point of Sale (POS) system designed specifically for restaurants, food stalls, cafés, and small to medium food businesses. It helps streamline daily operations by simplifying order management, sales tracking, and inventory control — all in one reliable system.

Built with efficiency and usability in mind, KusinaPOS empowers business owners to focus more on serving great food and customers, while the system handles the numbers behind the scenes.

Key Features

Fast and intuitive transactions
Menu and category management
Inventory and stock tracking
Sales summaries and reports
Offline local database support
Optimized for Android devices

KusinaPOS is continuously improved based on real-world restaurant workflows to ensure accuracy, speed, and ease of use.
